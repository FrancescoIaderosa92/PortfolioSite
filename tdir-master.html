Taxes Doing It Right Logo Rebrand

Final Product

The final product that was redesigned was nothing short of a success. This is my best piece in my portfolio.

Design

I chose this design because it best reflected the owner's background. It is essentially paying homage to the owner's family heritage.

Colour Choice

I chose the colour white, because the client wanted black business cards. having a black business card is more unique, because there are not a lot of people that have one.

Typeface

I chose this typeface because it is sans serif and easy to see when the type is small.

Target Audience

The target audience for this rebrand was to appeal to everyone over the age of 18. Almost everyone needs to get their taxes done, so I made a design that was easy for everyone to see.
